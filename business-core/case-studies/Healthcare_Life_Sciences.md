### F9: Johns Hopkins — AI Diagnostics (Vision AI)

**Client Type:** Academic Medical Research
**Role:** Lead Researcher / Collaborator
**Status:** ✅ Published (JAAD, 2025)

#### Problem
Evaluate whether GPT-4 Vision could assist dermatologists in diagnosing skin conditions from dermoscopic images — a critical question for AI-assisted medical diagnostics.

#### Solution
- Evaluated GPT-4 Vision's diagnostic accuracy on 273 biopsy-proven dermoscopic images
- Compared AI performance against 5 board-certified dermatologists
- Designed chain-of-thought prompting methodology with standardized JSON output
- Covered multiple skin conditions: melanoma, basal cell carcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma

#### Results
- **Published in Journal of the American Academy of Dermatology (JAAD), 2025**
- GPT-4V achieved 50.7% accuracy vs. 81.8% for human dermatologists
- Discovered significant over-diagnosis pattern (100% biopsy recommendation for benign cases vs. 37% by dermatologists)
- Established novel correlation between AI confidence scores and diagnostic precision
- Contributed to responsible AI integration framework for clinical dermatology

#### Technologies
GPT-4 Vision, chain-of-thought prompting, statistical analysis, dermoscopic image analysis

#### Team
Wei (lead researcher) + Johns Hopkins Department of Dermatology + 5 board-certified dermatologists

#### Sales Usage
**Use when:**
- Selling AI to healthcare/medical organizations
- Demonstrating research credibility (peer-reviewed publication)
- Proving ability to evaluate AI systems rigorously (not just build them)
- **Verticals:** Healthcare, diagnostics, medical imaging, research institutions

**Key proof point:** This is the "Johns Hopkins partnership" cited in the Executive Overview.

### F14: AI-Powered Medical Exam Question Generation

**Client Type:** Medical Education / Exam Prep
**Role:** Consultant via Insight Expansion
**Status:** ✅ Completed

#### Problem
A medical exam preparation platform needed to generate high-quality, novel exam questions at scale while maintaining clinical accuracy and appropriate difficulty levels.

#### Solution
- Fine-tuned domain-specific models (BioGPT, BioMedLM) on 10,000+ validated medical questions

#### Results
- **40% improvement in question relevance**
- **35% increase in difficulty accuracy**
- Scalable system serving 10,000+ platform users
- Reduced question generation from minutes to seconds
