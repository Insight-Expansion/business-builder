## Case Study 24: Dreamily Personalized Children's Books

**Client:** Nick (Australia-based Director)
**Client Type:** Publishing / E-commerce / Personalized Gifts
**Project:** AI-generated personalized children's books (physical, gift-wrapped delivery)
**Status:** ðŸ“ NDA to be signed, POC under consideration
**Budget:** ~$2K for MVP (content generation workflow)

### Problem
- Struggled 1.5 years to build technical backend
- Need consistent story generation and character creation
- **Biggest issue:** Character consistency across 15-20 pages (5-year-old Chloe changes appearance)
- Manual process currently (ChatGPT prompts, inconsistent Dall-E outputs)
- Wants physical product (printing + logistics network ready)

### Solution
User inputs memorable moment with child (e.g., beach trip, seagulls). AI generates whimsical story around that moment. Age-appropriate content (page count and writing style based on child's age). Pixar-style character imagery with consistency across pages (exploring better models than Dall-E). Format ready for printing (PDF handoff to printer).

**Future Features:**
- School market (anti-bullying, educational themes)
- Possible add-on: Animated video trailer of the book

### Technologies
- AI story generation (LLM)
- Character-consistent image generation (Midjourney/Stable Diffusion with LoRA/DreamBooth)
- Multi-page book layout
- Print-ready PDF generation

### Timeline
MVP ~$2K

### Team
Wei + creative AI specialists

### Sales Usage
Perfect for e-commerce personalization, gift businesses, parents, grandparents, anyone wanting authentic AI-powered physical products (counters "digital junk" trend). **Unique differentiator:** Physical product with authentic personalization - not just digital AI slop, creates meaningful keepsake gifts.

### Technical Challenges Solved

**Character Consistency:**
- Problem: DALL-E/Midjourney generates different-looking characters each page
- Solution: LoRA/DreamBooth fine-tuning on initial character design
- Benefit: 5-year-old Chloe looks like Chloe on all 20 pages

**Editorial Integrity:**
- Problem: LLMs hallucinate facts, mix up details
- Solution: Multi-agent fact-checking, RAG for source verification
- Benefit: Non-fiction books cite real sources, accurate information

**Print-Ready Outputs:**
- Problem: AI generates text/images, not designed layouts
- Solution: Automated PDF assembly with typography and pagination
- Benefit: Send directly to printer (no manual layout work)

**Age-Appropriate Content:**
- Problem: One-size-fits-all story complexity
- Solution: Dynamic page count and vocabulary based on child's age
- Benefit: 3-year-old gets simpler story than 8-year-old
