# Target Verticals & Ideal Client Profile (ICP)

**Purpose:** Define who we sell to, why they buy, and how to qualify them. Based on actual successful client engagements and sales conversations.

---

## Core ICP Characteristics (Cross-Vertical)

### Firmographic
- **Company Size:** 5-250 employees (SMB to mid-market)
- **Revenue:** $1M-50M annual
- **Stage:** Established (not pre-revenue startups unless strong funding)
- **Geography:** US primary, EU secondary, global opportunistic

### Technographic
- **Current State:** Manual processes ripe for automation
- **Tech Savvy:** Understands AI opportunity (not skeptics)
- **Existing Tools:** Using SaaS (CRM, project mgmt) but not fully leveraging

### Psychographic
- **Values:** Quality over cost (willing to pay for expertise)
- **Pain:** Drowning in manual work, can't scale without hiring
- **Mindset:** "Let's try this and see" not "Prove everything first"
- **Culture:** Treats people well (values teams, not just profit)

### Buying Behavior
- **Decision Speed:** 2-4 weeks (not 6-month procurement cycles)
- **Budget Authority:** Founder/owner or empowered executive
- **Risk Tolerance:** Medium-high (willing to try POC approach)
- **Previous Experience:** Often burned by previous devs (makes them cautious but motivated)

## Lead Qualification Framework

### Stage 1: Initial Screening (2 min)

**Qualifying Questions:**
1. "What problem are you trying to solve?"
   - ‚úÖ Clear problem statement
   - ‚ùå Vague "need an app" request

2. "What's your timeline?"
   - ‚úÖ 1-3 months
   - ‚ùå "Need it tomorrow" or "Whenever"

3. "What's your budget range?"
   - ‚úÖ $5K+ (or "don't know yet but willing to invest")
   - ‚ùå <$2K or "as cheap as possible"

4. "Have you worked with developers before?"
   - ‚úÖ Yes (understands process)
   - ‚ö†Ô∏è No (need more education)
   - ‚ùå Yes, and everyone sucks (red flag)

**Pass Stage 1?** ‚Üí Schedule 30-min discovery call

---

### Stage 2: Discovery Call (30 min)

**Assess:**
- **Problem Clarity:** Can they articulate the pain?
- **Budget Reality:** Do they understand rough costs?
- **Decision Authority:** Can they sign a contract?
- **Technical Readiness:** Do they have data, systems, access we need?
- **Culture Fit:** Are they pleasant to talk to?

**Green Flags:**
- ‚úÖ Prepared for call (reviewed our site, has questions)
- ‚úÖ Articulates ROI they expect
- ‚úÖ Asks about our experience/case studies
- ‚úÖ Has existing systems to integrate with
- ‚úÖ Respectful, collaborative tone

**Yellow Flags:**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è First time doing this (need more education)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Decision by committee (longer sales cycle)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Budget is tight (may need phasing)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Unclear requirements (POC will help clarify)

**Red Flags:**
- ‚ùå Arrogant, dismissive tone
- ‚ùå "Everyone else couldn't do this" (unrealistic expectations)
- ‚ùå Obsessed with IP protection (overly paranoid)
- ‚ùå "I'll decide if I pay after you build it" (wants free work)
- ‚ùå "I have 10 ideas, can you build all of them?" (unfocused)

**Pass Stage 2?** ‚Üí Commit to building POC

---

### Stage 3: POC Demo (30 min)

**Assess:**
- **Enthusiasm:** Are they excited by what we built?
- **Feedback Quality:** Constructive vs nitpicky?
- **Next Steps:** Do they want to move forward?
- **Price Reaction:** How do they respond to ballpark?

**Green Flags:**
- ‚úÖ "This is exactly what I need!"
- ‚úÖ Asks about timeline to full build
- ‚úÖ "What's the next step?"
- ‚úÖ Shares POC with their team (buying in)

**Red Flags:**
- ‚ùå "This isn't what I wanted" (requirements disconnect)
- ‚ùå "Can you add 10 more features before I decide?" (scope creep)
- ‚ùå Silent, no feedback (not engaged)

**Pass Stage 3?** ‚Üí Send proposal

---

### Stage 4: Proposal Review

**Assess:**
- **Response Time:** Do they review promptly?
- **Questions:** Substantive vs price-shopping?
- **Negotiation:** Reasonable vs impossible?

**Green Flags:**
- ‚úÖ Reviews within 2-3 days
- ‚úÖ Asks clarifying questions
- ‚úÖ "Can we start sooner?"

**Yellow Flags:**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Takes >1 week to review (not urgent)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Negotiates on price (may accept if reasonable)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Wants to "think about it" (need to probe)

**Red Flags:**
- ‚ùå Ghosts (probably went with competitor)
- ‚ùå "Way more expensive than I thought" (wasn't listening)
- ‚ùå Demands 50% discount (not serious)

## Lead Scoring System

### Assign Points:

**Budget (+30 points)**
- $20K+: +30
- $10-20K: +20
- $5-10K: +10
- <$5K: +0

**Urgency (+20 points)**
- Need in 2-4 weeks: +20
- Need in 1-2 months: +15
- Need in 3+ months: +5
- No timeline: +0

**Authority (+20 points)**
- Founder/owner: +20
- Empowered exec: +15
- Middle manager: +5
- No authority: +0

**Fit (+15 points)**
- Target vertical: +15
- Adjacent vertical: +10
- New vertical: +5
- Anti-ICP: +0

**Warmth (+15 points)**
- Referral: +15
- Upwork inbound: +10
- LinkedIn response: +5
- Cold outreach: +0

**Total: 100 points possible**

### Tiers:
- **80+ points:** A-tier lead (prioritize, fast response)
- **60-79 points:** B-tier lead (solid, standard process)
- **40-59 points:** C-tier lead (qualify carefully)
- **<40 points:** D-tier lead (deprioritize or decline)
