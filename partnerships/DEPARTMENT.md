## Key Processes

**1. Partner Onboarding** (2-4 weeks)
- Initial qualification ‚Üí Proposal ‚Üí Meeting ‚Üí Agreement ‚Üí Enablement

**2. Lead Introduction & Attribution**
- Partner identifies prospect ‚Üí Attribution notice ‚Üí Nexrizen confirms ‚Üí Introduction made

**3. Sales Process** (1-4 weeks)
- Discovery call ‚Üí Prototype build ‚Üí Review ‚Üí Proposal ‚Üí Negotiation ‚Üí Contract

**4. Commission Payment** (monthly + 30 days)
- Client payment received ‚Üí Calculate commission ‚Üí Monthly statement ‚Üí Payment within 30 days

**5. Partner Management** (ongoing)
- Monthly check-ins, quarterly reviews, annual renewal
