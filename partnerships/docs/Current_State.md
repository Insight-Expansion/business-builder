## Partnership Model Overview

### What It Is

Nexrizen has adopted an **authorized reseller / representative model** for expansion into EU/EEA markets. Partners identify and introduce prospective clients from their networks, and Nexrizen handles the technical sale, delivery, and support. Partners earn commission on all revenue from clients they introduce.

**Model type:** Non-exclusive reseller (initially)
**Compensation:** Success-fee based (15% commission)
**Partner role:** Relationship-based introductions, not technical selling

### Why This Model

**Reasons for choosing reseller approach:**
1. **Speed to market** - Leverage existing EU executive networks without building sales team
2. **Trust dynamics** - Warm introductions from trusted advisors have higher conversion than cold outreach
3. **Local knowledge** - Partners understand cultural, regulatory, and business norms in their territories
4. **Low overhead** - No salaries, office costs, or fixed expenses
5. **Aligned incentives** - Partners only earn when Nexrizen earns

**Quote from proposal:**
> "Your reputation with your network is paramount ‚Äî we understand that you are putting your name behind our work, and we take that seriously." ‚Äî `context/01_eu-partnership-proposal.md` lines 367-368

### What Partners Do (Scope)

**Authorized activities:**
- Identify potential clients from existing professional network
- Make warm introductions via email, call, or in-person meeting
- Describe Nexrizen's general capabilities using approved materials
- Participate in sales meetings and calls when appropriate
- Provide market context (industry norms, buyer expectations, competitive landscape)

**NOT authorized (per `context/04_nexrizen-reseller-agreement.md` ¬ß2.4):**
- Enter contracts on behalf of Nexrizen
- Quote specific prices without prior written approval
- Make representations beyond approved marketing materials
- Collect payments from clients
- Represent themselves as employees or agents with binding authority

### What Nexrizen Does

**Nexrizen responsibilities:**
- Run discovery calls and scope projects
- Create proposals and estimates
- Build and deliver working prototypes (often before contract)
- Handle all engineering, project management, and client communication during delivery
- Provide sales support (participate in calls, answer technical questions)
- Create and maintain approved marketing materials for partner use
- Track attribution and pay commissions
- Maintain confidentiality and professional standards

**Key advantage:**
> "Our internal operations run on the same AI systems we build for clients. We use proprietary tools that ingest every meeting, document, and communication to maintain perfect continuity across projects." ‚Äî `context/01_eu-partnership-proposal.md` lines 374-377

This AI-native operations model enables faster turnaround than traditional agencies.

### Attribution Rules

**How attribution is established:**
1. Partner sends email to Nexrizen identifying: company name, contact person, brief context
2. Nexrizen confirms receipt and attribution status within 5 business days
3. Attribution holds for 12 months from date of introduction
4. If Nexrizen has pre-existing relationship, partner is notified within 5 days (no commission)
5. If multiple sources introduce same client, attribution goes to first qualified introduction

**Source:** `context/04_nexrizen-reseller-agreement.md` ¬ß4

**"Qualified Introduction" definition (¬ß1.1):**
- Prospective client was not previously known to Nexrizen
- Partner provides written notice within 48 hours of introduction
- Prospect is bona fide business entity with decision-making authority

**Planned improvement:** Automated commission tracking system in Notion or dedicated tool
