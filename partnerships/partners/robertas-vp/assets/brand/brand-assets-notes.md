## Design Aesthetic

### Style Characteristics
- **Minimal and clean** - Focus on content, minimal decoration
- **Modern tech** - Gradients, subtle animations, rounded corners
- **Professional** - Blue/slate color scheme, serious but approachable
- **European-friendly** - Not loud or aggressive, sophisticated

### Common Design Patterns
- **Rounded corners:** `rounded-full`, `rounded-2xl`
- **Shadows:** `shadow-sm`, `shadow-lg`, `shadow-xl` with hover effects
- **Animations:** Subtle hover effects, blob animations for background elements
- **Gradients:** Used extensively for backgrounds and text
- **Grid layouts:** 2-3-4 column responsive grids

### Spacing
- **Padding:** `py-24` (vertical sections), `px-6 lg:px-8` (horizontal)
- **Gaps:** `gap-6 lg:gap-8` for grids
- **Max width:** `max-w-6xl` or `max-w-7xl` for content containers

## Design Guidelines for EU One-Pager

### Do's
✅ Use clean sans-serif typography (Inter)
✅ Stick to navy/blue/white color scheme
✅ Use subtle gradients for visual interest
✅ Keep backgrounds light and minimal
✅ Use rounded corners for modern feel
✅ Emphasize metrics with gradient text
✅ Maintain generous white space

### Don'ts
❌ No stock photos (use abstract visuals or icons)
❌ No loud colors or aggressive design
❌ No complex patterns or busy backgrounds
❌ No multiple accent colors (stick to blue family)
❌ No tracking pixels (GDPR compliance)
