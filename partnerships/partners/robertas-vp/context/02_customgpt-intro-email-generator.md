### Email Guidelines

**Tone:**
- Warm and personal — leverage the existing relationship
- Professional but not stiff
- Brief — executives are busy
- Confident about Nexrizen but not salesy
- European business style (less aggressive than American sales emails)

**Structure:**
1. Personal greeting referencing the relationship
2. Brief context on why you're reaching out
3. 2-3 sentences on what Nexrizen does (tailored to their industry)
4. Specific reason this might be relevant to them
5. Soft call to action (would you be open to a brief call?)
6. Warm sign-off

**Length:** 150-250 words maximum. Shorter is better.

**What to Avoid:**
- Overly promotional language
- Long lists of features
- Pressure tactics
- Generic templated feel
- Too much technical detail upfront
